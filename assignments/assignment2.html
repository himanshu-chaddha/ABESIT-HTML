My Favourite Pokemons
Sr. Pokemon Generation Evolve To Description
1. Pichu Gen II Pikachu Pichu are very small, plump, yellow rodent-like Pokémon. It bears a similar appearance to its evolved form Pikachu, but varies in a number of ways.Pichu have diamond-shaped ears on the top of their head, with the edges being black and the interior yellow.
2. Torchic Gen III Combusken Torchic is a small, chick-like Pokémon. Its soft, fluffy plumage is mainly bright orange, with tiny, yellow, developing wings, and a yellow plume of three feathers atop its head. It has a small, tan beak, and large, black eyes. It has tan-colored talons with three digits.
3. Piplup Gen VI Prinplup Piplup is a light-blue penguin-like Pokémon, which is covered in thick down to insulate against the cold. It has a dark blue head with a primarily white face and a short, yellow beak. The dark blue feathers on its head extend down its back and around its neck, which causes it to appear to be wearing a cape.
4. Cosmoem Gen VII Solgaleo Legendary Pokémon introduced in Generation VII. It is the evolved form of Cosmog and the pre-evolved form of Solgaleo (in Sun) and Lunala (in Moon).
5. Zamazenta Gen VIII Crowned Shield Legendary Pokémon introduced in Generation VIII. It is the mascot for Pokémon Shield. When it turns into its Crowned Shield form, it is a Fighting/Steel-type.
